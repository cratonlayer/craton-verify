craton.receipt.protocol.v1

Verify a Craton receipt without calling Craton.

Paste a retained receipt and a pinned JWKS key bundle. This page verifies receipt.signature over the decoded receipt.payload_b64 bytes exactly, then displays the signed payload.

Paste a receipt, {"receipt": ...}, or a full boundary response.

For production receipts, use the JWKS you retained from /protocol/v1/jwks.json.

No receipt verified yet.

The verifier selects the public key whose kid matches the receipt, decodes payload_b64, and verifies the Ed25519 signature over those exact bytes. It does not reserialize JSON and does not make network requests.
